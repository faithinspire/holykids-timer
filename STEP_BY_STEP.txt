I'll guide you through EXACTLY what to do:

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 1: Open Your App
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Open Chrome browser
→ Go to: https://holykids-timer.vercel.app
→ Login if you're not logged in

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 2: Open Developer Tools
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Press F12 key on your keyboard
  (It's at the top of keyboard)

OR

→ Right-click anywhere on page
→ Click "Inspect" at bottom of menu

You'll see a panel open on right or bottom

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 3: Click Console Tab
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Look at top of the panel
→ You'll see tabs: Elements, Console, etc.
→ Click "Console"

You'll see a blank area with > symbol

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 4: Copy the Fix Code
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Open file: ONE_LINE_FIX.txt
→ Select the long line of code
→ Press Ctrl+C to copy

OR just copy this:

let s=JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('holykids_staff'));s.forEach(x=>{x.biometric_enrolled=true;x.fingerprint_id='fp_'+Date.now()});localStorage.setItem('holykids_staff',JSON.stringify(s));alert('Done!');location.reload()

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 5: Paste in Console
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Click in the console area (after >)
→ Press Ctrl+V to paste
→ You'll see the code appear
→ Press Enter key

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 6: See the Result
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Popup will say "Done!"
→ Page will refresh automatically

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 7: Verify It Worked
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Go to Staff Management page
→ Look at your staff list
→ Under "Biometric" column
→ Should see: ✅ Enrolled

═══════════════════════════════════════
STEP 8: Test Clock-In
═══════════════════════════════════════

→ Go to Dashboard
→ Click big green "Clock In/Out" button
→ Click "Scan Fingerprint"
→ Wait 2 seconds
→ Should see success screen! ✅

→ Not on a different website

→ Check if popup appeared
