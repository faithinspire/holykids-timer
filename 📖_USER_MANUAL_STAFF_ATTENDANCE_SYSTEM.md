## 1. INTRODUCTION

### 1.1 Purpose of This Manual
This manual provides step-by-step instructions for using the Staff Attendance Management System. It is designed for both staff members and administrators.

### 1.2 What is the Attendance System?
The Staff Attendance Management System is a digital solution that allows staff to clock in and out using their personal PIN or device fingerprint. The system automatically records attendance, generates reports, and helps management track staff presence.

### 1.3 Key Benefits
- **Fast Clock-In:** Takes less than 10 seconds
- **Secure:** Uses PIN and biometric authentication
- **Accurate:** Automatic time recording with no manual entry
- **Accessible:** Works on any device with internet
- **Paperless:** All records stored digitally

## 4. FOR STAFF MEMBERS

### 4.1 First-Time Setup

#### Step 1: Receive Your Staff ID
Your administrator will provide you with:
- Staff ID Number (e.g., STF0001)
- Temporary access credentials

#### Step 2: Set Up Your PIN

1. Contact your administrator to initiate fingerprint setup
2. Administrator will send you a setup link
3. Click the link to open the Fingerprint Setup page
4. **Create Your Personal PIN:**
   - Enter a 4-6 digit PIN that you'll remember
   - Confirm your PIN by entering it again
   - Click "Set PIN & Continue"

**PIN Requirements:**
- Must be 4-6 digits
- Must be unique (no two staff can have the same PIN)
- Keep it confidential
- Easy to remember but hard for others to guess

**Example Good PINs:**
- 2468 (simple pattern)
- 1990 (birth year)
- 0815 (memorable date)

**Example Bad PINs:**
- 1234 (too common)
- 0000 (too simple)
- Your staff number (too obvious)

#### Step 3: Register Your Fingerprint

After setting your PIN:

1. Click "Register Fingerprint" button
2. Your device will prompt you to use your fingerprint sensor
3. Place your finger on the sensor (phone/laptop fingerprint reader)
4. Follow device instructions
5. Wait for "‚úÖ Fingerprint registered successfully!" message

**Note:** This registers your device's biometric authentication, not your actual fingerprint data.

## 7. TROUBLESHOOTING

### 7.1 Common Issues and Solutions

#### Issue: "Invalid PIN" Error

**Cause:** Incorrect PIN entered

**Solution:**
1. Try entering PIN again carefully
2. Ensure you're using the correct PIN
3. If forgotten, contact administrator for reset

#### Issue: "Fingerprint Not Recognized"

**Cause:** Fingerprint not registered or sensor issue

**Solution:**
1. Use PIN method instead
2. Clean your fingerprint sensor
3. Try a different finger
4. Re-register fingerprint with administrator

#### Issue: "Already Clocked In Today"

**Cause:** You've already clocked in for today

**Solution:**
- This is normal - you can only clock in once per day
- If this is an error, contact administrator

#### Issue: "Failed to Record Attendance"

**Cause:** Database connection issue

**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try again in a few seconds
3. If persists, contact administrator
4. Administrator may need to run database update

#### Issue: App Not Loading

**Cause:** Internet connection or browser issue

**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
3. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+R)
4. Try different browser
5. Restart device

#### Issue: Can't See Reports

**Cause:** Not logged in as administrator

**Solution:**
- Only administrators can access reports
- Contact your administrator if you need report data

### 7.2 Browser Cache Issues

**When to Clear Cache:**
- After system updates
- When seeing old/outdated information
- When features aren't working

**How to Clear Cache:**

**Chrome:**
1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Click "Clear data"

**Safari:**
1. Press Cmd+Option+E
2. Or go to Safari ‚Üí Clear History

**Firefox:**
1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete
2. Select "Cache"
3. Click "Clear Now"

## 8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### 8.1 General Questions

**Q: Do I need to install an app?**  
A: No, the system works in your web browser. Just bookmark the URL.

**Q: Can I use my phone?**  
A: Yes! The system works on any device with internet and a web browser.

**Q: Is my fingerprint data stored?**  
A: No. The system only stores a secure credential ID from your device. Your actual fingerprint is never stored.

**Q: What if I don't have a fingerprint sensor?**  
A: You can use PIN-only method. Fingerprint is optional.

**Q: Can I clock in from home?**  
A: Yes, if your organization allows remote clock-in. Check with your administrator.

### 8.2 For Staff

**Q: How do I know if I clocked in successfully?**  
A: You'll see a green "‚úÖ Clocked in successfully!" message.

**Q: Can I change my PIN?**  
A: Yes, contact your administrator to reset your PIN.

**Q: What if someone else knows my PIN?**

**Q: Can I clock in for someone else?**
